Understanding TRAICE

TRAICE (Tactical Recursive AI for Capital Efficiency) enables AI systems to continuously enhance their own learning and performance. They apply their own logic to self-improve, becoming more efficient and effective over time.

The TRAICE Self-Improvement Loop

1. AI Action & Output
↓
2. Collect Feedback (Performance, Errors, Critiques)
↓
3. AI Self-Refines (Updates Logic, Prompts, Parameters via TRAICE)
↺ (to Step 1)

This iterative cycle allows TRAICE-enabled AI to adapt, solve complex problems, and improve resourcefully.
